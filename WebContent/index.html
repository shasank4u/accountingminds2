Welcome to the Accounting World


Career opportunities in accounting and finance are endless. With an apt finance qualification one can specialize in any of its branches, and can build a promising career. Some of the branches of specialization are :


  • Accounting
  • Auditing
  • Corporate Finance
  • Corporate Recovery
  • Financial Services
  • Stock Broking
  • Taxation Management
  • Actuary Services

Whichever specialization one may choose, he/she needs to be backed by strong finance and accounting education background. There is no other qualification in finance that equips you with strong base of accounting and finance knowledge than a CA or ICWAI Qualification These are well recognized qualifications across countries and open career possibilities with large corporation globally.
