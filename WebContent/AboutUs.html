The Founders

Accounting Minds is a high spirited initiative of Phaneendra and Surya. The goal is to extend coaching and guidance to commerce students pursuing CA, CMA and CS with a belief that success in CA, CMA and CS is easy if one understands, studies prepares with a holistic and practical approach.

The founders have international exposure on accounting systems and business organizations and they look forward in sharing their knowledge with young, vibrant CA and CMA aspirants.

They pursued their education from Orissa, having limited access to professional coaching institutes. From the start, by the strength of their confidence and determination they have achieved meritorious results in academics and career.

Phaneendra

A Qualified Chartered Accountant and Cost Accountant. He pursued CA and CMA courses in remote part of India, without attending any class room coaching. He is currently working with a noted MNC (A fortune 500 company) as a Senior Manager. He has a total 8 Years of teaching experience in commerce including 3 years of experience in teaching CA and CMA courses. He was also a visiting faculty at ICAI Bangalore Chapter.
